How to Play Wordle

Objective

Guess the 4-6-letter word in as few attempts as possible.

Rules

  • You have 6 attempts to guess the secret word.
  • Each guess must fill the blank tiles.
  • Each guess must be a valid word in the dictionary.
  • Do not guess the same word more than once.

Gameplay

  1. You are provided with a hint.
  2. Start by entering your guess into the tiles.
  3. Submit your guess by pressing the Enter or key.
  4. Receive feedback on your guess based on the color of the tile:
    • Green: if the letter is in the word, and it is in the correct spot.
    • Yellow: if the letter is in the word, but it is not in the correct spot.
    • Grey: if the letter is not in the word.
  5. Continue guessing until you correctly identify the word or run out of attempts.
