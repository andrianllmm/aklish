About Aklish

Welcome to Aklish, your platform for crowdsourcing Aklanon-English translations and fostering language learning and cultural exchange.

Our Mission

At Aklish, our mission is to bridge language barriers and celebrate linguistic diversity by empowering Aklanon speakers and foreign learners to contribute and engage with the Aklanon and English languages.

Features

  • Crowdsourced Translations
    Aklish allows users to add text entries for translation or contribute their translations to existing text entries.
  • Aklanon-English Dictionary
    Explore our comprehensive dictionary to look up Aklanon and English words, complete with definitions, parts of speech, examples, and more!
  • Proofreading Tools
    Our integrated proofreading tools help users refine their Aklanon and English writing skills, offering spelling suggestions tailored to both languages.
  • Language Games
    Aklish offers a variety of interactive games like Wordle and Match designed to reinforce language skills and vocabulary retention.
