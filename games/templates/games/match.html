How to Play Match

Objective

Choose the word that is similar to the word being asked.

Rules

  • You have only one try to pick the matching word.
  • You can only choose among the provided options.

Gameplay

  1. You are provided with a word and four choices.
  2. Select a word from the provided options that is similar to the word being asked.
  3. Click or tap on your chosen option.
  4. Receive immediate feedback on your choice.
  5. The button of the correct choice will turn green, while the button of the wrong choice will turn red.
